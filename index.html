The local area

Langs beach is a beautiful family friendly beach with safe swimming and plenty of uncrowded sand with gorgeous pohutukawa shade. Walking tracks go north along the rugged coastline to Waipu Cove (1Hr walk) or south across farmland then an elevated hilltop path with stunning views to Mangawhai Heads (2Hr walk).

Visit the local weekend markets at Mangawhai (10 Mins drive) or Waipu (7 minutes drive). Dine at the Mangawhai Tavern, Cove or Dune restaurants, McLeods’s Pizza Barn or any of the other venues all within 10 minutes drive. Or wander down to the beachfront each morning to grab a Zippy’s coffee or real fruit icecream. Boat launching is at Mangawhai Heads. Local Langs beach boat launching requires a tractor, and there are plenty of these occupying the high tide mark during summer. You can gather pipis at low tide at Langs, catch snapper from the beach, snorkel or dive the coastline, surf at Johnson’s Point or Waipu, or just kick back and relax at Langs.
